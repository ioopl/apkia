iOS Accessibility-Focused Development

We specialize in building iOS applications that are fully compliant with:

  • WCAG: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
  • EAA: European Accessibility Act
  • ADA: Americans with Disabilities Act

From VoiceOver Annotations integration to Large Dynamic Type Font scaling, Dark Mode support we ensure your apps are compliant to International standards and are usable by everyone.
We use these tools like Mac Accessibility Inspector for iOS to check for various iOS accessibility components
- Large Content Viewer for Custom Views,
- Custom Rotors to simplify complex VoiceOver Accessibility navigation situations.
- Snapshot Tests to ensure UI (including Fonts) scales to support Larger Accessibility Sizes.
- Writing Contextual VoiceOver Labels for all Views.
- Checking the Contrast Ratio for colours of any UI element/Font are 1:4.5 to cater for legibility for the Low Vision/Colour Blind users.
all this and many more ways make sure the Apps adhere to WCAG 2.2 AA and WIA-ARIA compliance, with VoiecOver JAWS and NVDA.

Accessibility Quality Assurance Testing

We Audit the Apps to suggest improvements in order to improve Accessibility experience and ensuring that it doesn't exclude users who have different disabilities or interact with their devices in certain different ways. We ensure the content of the Apps is POUR: Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, and Robust. We provide in-depth QA services for mobile apps focused on accessibility testing:

  • Conformance with WCAG 2.2 AA standards
  • Compliance with EAA and ADA legal frameworks
  • Automated and manual testing of VoiceOver, Switch Controls, and color contrast

Receive actionable audit reports and support through remediation.


For Auditing we may use tools like Evinced SDK or Manual and Automated UI tests to detect the Accessibility issues.
