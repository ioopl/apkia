IT Accessibility Consultancy Services

We can become your partner in inclusive Mobile Application development and testing. We provide auditing for your iOS and visionOS Apps to discover accessibility issues. We audit mobile Apps based on:

  • EN 301 549 compliance
  • WCAG 2.1 – Level AA
  • EU Directive 2016/2102
  • AgID accessibility guidelines (Italy)
  • Ley 11/2023 (Spain)
  • Generating a formal VPAT/ACR - Voluntary Product Accessibility Template/Accessibility Conformance Report (covers WCAG, Revised Section 508, and EU/EN 301 549) for Mobile apps (iOS/Android)
This auditing can accelerate your user research, design and development stages by making sure it complies with WCAG and EAA standards. The auditing service includes manual and automation accessibility testing (Quality Assurance), remediation guidance and iOS development.

Service 1 - iOS Accessibility Development

We offer development services in building your new (or migrating an existing) iOS & visionOS applications so that they are fully compliant with:

  • WCAG: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
  • EAA: European Accessibility Act
  • ADA: Americans with Disabilities Act

As part of this service we will conduct accessibility audits and evaluations of apple-based platforms. From VoiceOver Annotations integration to Large Dynamic Type Font scaling, Eye Tracking Support, Dark Mode support we ensure your apps are compliant to International standards and are usable by everyone. We use real in-house devices and latest tools in Xcode (like Mac Accessibility Inspector) to check for various iOS, iPadOS and visionOS accessibility issues. We create clear, actionable remediation recommendations such as :

  • Checking Contextual VoiceOver labels for all Views
  • Checking the Contrast Ratio for colours of any UI element/Font are 1:4.5 to cater for legibility for the Low Vision/Colour Blind users
  • Implementing Large Content Viewer for Custom Views
  • Custom Rotors to simplify complex VoiceOver navigation scenarios
  • Snapshot Tests to ensure UI (including Fonts) scales to support Larger Accessibility Sizes
    • all this and many more accessibility features to make sure your Apps adhere to WCAG 2.2 AA and WIA-ARIA compliance

Service 2 - iOS Accessibility Quality Assurance (QA)/Testing

We offer Quality Assurance services by auditing you existing Apps or Codebase to diagnose accessibility issues and suggest improvements in order to improve User Experience (UX). This auditing ensures that your Apps do not exclude the users who have different abilities or interact with their devices in certain different ways. We ensure the content of the Apps is POUR: Perceivable, Operable, Understandable and Robust. We provide in-depth QA services for mobile apps focused on accessibility testing criteria such as :

  • Conformance with WCAG 2.2 AA standards
  • Compliance with EAA and ADA legal frameworks
  • Automated and manual testing of VoiceOver, Dark Mode, Landscape Mode, Switch Controls and Color contrast
  • Receive actionable audit reports and support through remediation
  • Clear bug reports, test plans and test cases in JIRA, Google sheets or relevant tools
For Auditing we rely on Manual and Automation Testing (covered via Automated UI tests to detect the Accessibility issues).
We can also recommend and implement tools like Evinced SDK to discover accessibility issues which are hard to detect using Manual or Automation tests.
